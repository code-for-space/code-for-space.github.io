{{< /blocks/cover >}} {{% blocks/lead color="primary" %}} code_for_space is a group of volunteer developers, engineers, & citizen scientists developing open-source projects for the advancement of space science and space exploration! Featured projects can include, but not limited to: * NASA/JPL Open Source Rover: 1/6th scale open-source design of the Mars Curiosity rover. * Project PANOPTES: Citizen-science robotic telescopes that can detect transiting exoplanets * Sloan Digital Sky Survey: detailed three-dimensional maps of the Universe, with deep multi-color images of one-third of the sky and spectra for more than three million astronomical objects. * Celestial: Real-time 3D visualization of space * Stellarium: A realistic, real-time 3D simulation of the night sky * KubOS: Open source software stack for satellites * Space Decentral: A decentralized platform for developing and funding open-source space missions and projects {{% /blocks/lead %}} {{< blocks/section color="dark" >}} {{% blocks/feature icon="none" %}} {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fab fa-github" title="Contributions welcome!" url="https://github.com/code-for-space" %}} {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{< /blocks/section >}}
